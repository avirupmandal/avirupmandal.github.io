Biography

I am currently pursuing my MTech-PhD dual degree in Communication Engineering from Electrical Engineering Department of Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay). I am fortunate to have two amazing professors, Dr. Parag Chaudhuri and Dr. Subhasis Chaudhuri, as my supervisors. I completed my B.E. in Electronics and Tele-Communication Engineering from Jadavpur University, Kolkata in 2015. I am currently working on Fast Fracture Simulation With Haptic Feedback.
